# 💼 Sales Insights Dashboard – Computer Hardware Business

### 📊 Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance for computer hardware business

---

## 🧾 Project Overview
This project focuses on a **computer hardware company** that is struggling to adapt to a dynamically changing market.  
To address this, the **Sales Director** decided to invest in a **Power BI-based data analysis project** to gain real-time insights into sales performance, profitability, and customer trends.  

The objective of this project is to:
- Analyze historical sales data across regions, customers, and products  
- Identify high-performing and underperforming markets  
- Evaluate customer contribution and profit margins  
- Support strategic decision-making through data visualization

## 🧩 Dataset Information

The data is sourced from the **Sales Insights Database** containing the following tables:

| **Table Name** | **Description** |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Customers** | Customer details such as ID, name, and region |
| **Products** | Product details including ID, category, and sub-category |
| **Markets** | Market or region information where the company operates |
| **Sales** | Transactional sales data with revenue, quantity, and profit |
| **Date** | Time dimension for monthly, quarterly, and yearly analysis |

## 📈 Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** ₹985 Million  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 2 Million Units  
- **Total Profit Margin:** ₹25 Million  
- **Analysis Period:** 2017–2020

## 🔍 Insights and Recommendations

### **Insight 1: Overall Performance**
The company generated ₹985M revenue with a 2.5% profit margin.  
**Recommendation:** Improve cost efficiency and pricing to enhance profitability.

---

### **Insight 2: Market Dominance**
Delhi NCR contributes 52.8% of total revenue, showing high dependence on one region.  
**Recommendation:** Expand operations in Mumbai, Ahmedabad, and Bhopal to diversify sales.

---

### **Insight 3: Profitability by Region**
Mumbai and Bhopal show strong profits, while Bengaluru records losses (-20.8%).  
**Recommendation:** Optimize or withdraw from low-performing regions to reduce losses.

---

### **Insight 4: Revenue Trend**
Revenue peaked in 2018 but declined by 2020.  
**Recommendation:** Revive demand through new campaigns and product bundling strategies.

---

### **Insight 5: Customer Dependence**
Electricalsara Stores contributes 42% of total revenue.  
**Recommendation:** Broaden customer base and incentivize medium-tier clients to reduce risk.

---

### **Insight 6: Negative Margin Customers**
Some customers have negative profit margins.  
**Recommendation:** Reassess pricing and credit policies for such customers.

---

### **Insight 7: High-Performing Regions**
Bhopal, Patna, and Surat show high profit potential.  
**Recommendation:** Scale operations and marketing in these high-margin areas.
